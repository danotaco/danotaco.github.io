About Me

I design experiences that get users what they want from your websites and apps.

User Experience Designer, UX Lead

Dan Owens

Always mindful of the big picture and keeping it simple, Dan steers opinion out of design direction by crafting solutions backed by user research and data.

He collaborates with teams and clients to institute user-centered design principles across the organization, creating interactive prototypes to avoid development rework and save stakeholder money.

What I Do

Interaction Design

I create (and re-use!) design patterns most suitable for your audience, architecting scalable design elements and high fidelity wireframes.

Rapid Prototyping

I build clickable interactive prototypes and choreograph screenflows end-to-end to validate designs with users and gain stakeholder buy-in.

User Research

From contextual interviews to moderated usability testing, I plan and facilitate the activities needed for me to make informed design recommendations.

Information Architecture

I plan navigation paradigms and screenflows that marry the breadth and depth of your content with how your users expect to find information.

Let's work Together

From Wall Street to Washington, Dan's interaction design and UX lead work spans a range of industries and personas, creating experiences through digital products, websites, and mobile apps.
